dot.

more than just a social media platform.


dot was created to solve a very simple yet pervasive problem: there's way too much negativity on social media.

too much noise. too much outrage. too much hatred and bigotry. there's not enough kindness. not enough listening. not enough curiosity, empathy, joy. not enough connection. not enough humanity.

a far cry from the hellish doomscrolling of many other sites, dot is a quieter, kinder space for people who still believe the Internet can be good. it's a safe space to post about anything and everything (as long as it's positive!) — a food you've been craving, the song you've been listening to on repeat for the past three days, your celebrity crush (or your real-life crush), a small victory at work, or just a moment that made your day a little brighter.

welcome to your daily dose of good vibes: no more noise, no more negativity, no more trolls, no more algorithms, no more drama, no more endless doomscrolling. just real connections with real people, real empathy, real joy, and real happiness.

real humanity.
